SHE DOES FOUNDATION

The SHEDOES FOUNDATION is a charitable platform that aims at empowering teenagers (girls and boys). Its Objectives:

  1. Early Pregnancy and Teenage Contraceptives
  2. Mentorship and Leadership
  3. Drug and Substance Abuse
  4. Education and Career Opportunities
  5. GBV and Mental Health
  6. Self-awareness and body grooming
  7. Goal to clean and green environment.
